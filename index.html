My main responsibilities are to respond to issues raised by clients, fixing problems and system bugs, as well as developing new features to be included and have recently taken the lead role in the recent roll out of version 13 of the software.

Whilst Complete Data uses the FileMaker platform, I have spent time with a sister product, WebBooks, developing solutions in PHP, connecting to their MySQL databases.

I have spent time volunteering at the Black Dog Institute as a website developer, where I rebuilt their intranet. I have also provided website support to the HAE Australasia advocacy group. Both, the BDI and HAE solutions utilised the Drupal CMS. I also led the design, development and support of the LifeSpan Suicide Prevention Confluence site, used internally for collaboration between sites.

Building applications in my own time is something I enjoy, for mobiles, web, and desktop.

I am a proven fast learner, fully capable of working alone and self-managing but thrive working as part of, and contributing to, the success of a team. Time management skills mean I can prioritise projects and work to deadlines. With exceptional spoken and written English, as well as a working fluency of Brazilian Portuguese, an attention to detail, and effective motivational and organisational skills, matched with a desire to learn, improve, and better myself, I believe I would be a valuable addition to any team and organisation.
